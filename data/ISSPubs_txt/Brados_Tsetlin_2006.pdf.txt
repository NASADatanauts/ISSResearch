[Some results of radiaton monitoring of the ISS Russian Segment in 2000-2005].
[Article in Russian]
Tsetlin W, Akatov luA, Arkhangel'ski‘t W, Mitrikas VG, Bondarenko VA, Makin SA.
Abstract '
Dynamics of the ISS RS radiation environment was studied using the data of daily (operative) monitoring and personal
dose measurements during 11 increments in the period between August 1, 2000 and October 28, 2005 overlapping the
maximum phase of the 23rd solar cycle. It was shown that personal absorbed doses varied within the range of
measurements of dual wavelength radiometer R-16, a component of the ISS radiation monitoring system. Power of the
absorbed doses fell in the range of 0.017 to 0.02 cGy/day and was solar—dependent.
